Key Insights
Concept stickiness — 82% of reached merchants recalled after just 3 days. The field pitch leaves a mark; most remember “something about investment or gold.”
Group invite = weak lever — only 2 of 9 who recalled (22%) joined. Declines were about the channel, not the concept. One merchant liked digital gold but had no time for a group; another hung up the moment the group was mentioned.
Bright spots — Ashok: frustrated by gold unavailability and black market, sees digital gold as a solution. Bashir: curiosity-driven, eager to explore and learn.
6 unreached — All calls were one evening session (6–11pm). Retry during shop hours (10am–2pm).
🎯
Verdict: 22% passed all 3 gates (2/9 recalled) — below the ≥30% demand threshold. Recall is strong (82%), but the group invite needs a stronger value prop or a different channel.
