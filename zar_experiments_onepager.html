What We Learned
Critical Finding: Demos work exceptionally well (80% conversion), BUT we have an opener problem blocking us from reaching the demo stage.

✅ Demos Are Highly Effective

  • Bank transfer demos: Perfect conversion
  • POS demos: 75% success rate
  • $5 golden flow: 86% conversion

❌ Opener Stage Is The Blocker

  • Only 10% pass opener (38/379 visits)
  • Only 66% reach demo after passing opener
  • Ambassadors derailing into product talk instead of moving to demo
Key Issue: Ambassadors using "I explained ZAR" trick opener (planted to catch non-compliance). They either derail into product talk or answer questions instead of moving to demo. Previous opener selection data is now unreliable.

What We Learned
Caveat: Success appears driven by personal relationships with stores, not necessarily by understanding or effectively communicating the ZAR value proposition.

🎯 Performance

  • 100% onboarding rate (5/5)
  • All stores had existing relationships
  • Confidence in getting customers

💰 Store Expectations

  • All 5 stores asked for target + reward structure
  • Wanted incentives beyond $10 for first $30 sold
  • Owais confident if reward is high enough
Next Steps:
  • Track Owais's store performance over next few days
  • Conduct shadow buyer visits to validate actual customer experience
  • Shadow Owais on visits to validate store understanding and pitch quality
  • Assess whether success is replicable beyond personal relationships

Key Takeaways

✅ What's Working

  • Demos convert at 80% rate
  • Bank transfer demos: 100% success
  • Smartphone + POS stores: 30% conversion
  • Relationship-based selling works

❌ What Needs Fixing

  • Opener stage is the critical blocker (10% pass rate)
  • Ambassadors derailing into product talk
  • Technical issues with bank transfers and card topups
