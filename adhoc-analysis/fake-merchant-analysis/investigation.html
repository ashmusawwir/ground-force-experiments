Next-Generation Signals — PA7

Core Insight The Signal Pivot

Every signal tested in PA1–PA6 measured merchant behavior. The fraud is ambassador-side. New signals must measure ambassador behavioral anomalies across their portfolio, not any individual merchant's profile.

The merchant in a fraud scenario is a ghost, a complicit shell, or an unwitting participant — all three look identical in the merchant's DB record. The signal is in the ambassador's pattern across their portfolio.

Sources: Terra (field ops), Nash (game theory), FIELD_FRAUD researcher, WEB_RESEARCH (GSMA 2024, MicroSave, Featurespace/Feedzai, Finextra, VARInsights)

What survives as operationally meaningful

1. Cross-ambassador split-attribution: ilyas_khan receives PE enrollment credit for merchants physically visited by shah comm. Whether this is collusion or coincidence requires checking if the two ambassadors are co-located or share a team.
2. Enrollment-before-physical-demo timing: Confirmed for hamza (enrolled 6 min before demo visit). Fully DB+visit-sheet queryable. Needs fleet-wide check across all ambassadors.
3. Amplitude absence (3/10 merchants): Cleanest Amplitude signal available. Misses confirmed fakes because the ambassador operated their phones. Useful for detecting a different type of fake (zero device interaction), not the ambassador-operated variety.
4. GPS co-location at enrollment: gujjar at 0.007 km and hamza at 0.103 km corroborate ambassador device operation. Strengthen the case when combined with other signals; not standalone.
5. PA7-F — Off-hours enrollments (FIRES): ilyas_khan enrolled 70% of merchants outside legitimate field hours (3am–9am or after 7pm PKT). Third highest in fleet behind happy_griffin_1631 (100%) and junaid9778 (85.7%). Threshold: 30%. Signal fires clearly and is fleet-scalable via off_hours_enrollment_query().
6. PA7-C/D — Visit-to-Enrollment Ratio (FIRES): ilyas_khan has zero visits logged in the formal ambassador tracking system against 9 PE enrollments — VER = 0. Every other ambassador in the fleet has at least some logged visits. This is the most extreme VER in the dataset. The formal visit tracking sheet (499 rows, 8 ambassadors via email match) confirms ilyas_khan never submitted a single visit log, while actively accruing enrollment credits.
PA7 signals that did not fire: Device fingerprint dedup (PA7-A — all 7 unique), DCN burst pattern (PA7-B — max 2, normal), enrollment timing variance (PA7-E — below daily threshold), demo note distribution (PA7-I — fleet-normal). PA7-H (leaf node) needs refinement to exclude same-day demo-fund bank transfers before it can fire.
