📢 Broadcast Log tab — when a flyer is posted

Separate tab — one row per flyer post. Tracks where we posted and how many EOIs each group produced.
ColumnWhat to fill
Date PostedDate flyer was posted

Daily workflow

Afsar receives EOI on WhatsApp → fills City, Name, Phone, Source on Feb 18 Onwards tab
Afsar logs each flyer post on the Broadcast Log tab — group name, type, est. members, EOIs received
Qasim interviews candidate in person → fills Interview Date + 4 evaluation dropdowns + notes. Row turns yellow.
Qasim runs training session → fills Training Date, Showed Up, Pass/Fail. Row turns green or red.
Day 7 retention is tracked automatically from the visits sheet — no action needed here.
